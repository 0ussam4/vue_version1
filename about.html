About Our Brand

Equinoxe Records is an independent musical label, record label and music production house, exclusively focused on young artists and fresh talent. We spend our waking days in studios, production houses, festivals and music venues, trying to enlist the best artists out there, and assist them in their respective musical journeys.

We do not make our artists sign any deals: Independance is an important aspect in the music business, and our agents are well aware of it. You get to keep your masters, and we work tirelessly to make your music known to new ears around the globe. And as a bonus, we also work for your rights and your money. Everything is in your control!

Your profits are yours to keep, our profits go to charity: Every month, our pool of artists choose one single charity to which Equinoxe will send the totality of its profits. We only manage the operational costs, the rest makes the world a better place!

“Equinoxe Music is made by artists, for artists. But no billionnaire was abused during the making of this label.”
Shawn Corey Carter, aka. Jay-Z
